About

I'm Pretty Awesome

For as long as I can remember I have been interested in computers and what makes them work. Whether it was building my own computers from scratch, building Arduino robots, coding my own custom shell scripts, to developing my very own web apps; I am passionate about understanding the inner workings of all things technical at a fundamental level. I am currently perusing a second degree in computer science, at Portland State University, and exploring web development on my own time. Most Recently, I have graduated from Portland Code Schools Full Stack JavaScript program, where I spent several months immersed in the language of JavaScript and its many frameworks.

On my off time, I enjoy board games, hiking, kayaking, and I am always playing with code. Most recently I am building a Magic The Gathering wish list phone app, that will allow me, and fellow enthusiast to track prices of their favorite cards, and even purchase them from their phone!
